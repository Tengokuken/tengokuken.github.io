DFI ChatBot

Along with 3 other students, we created a functional chat bot for the FinTech Chatbot Challenge between the Digital Finance Institute and the University of Toronto. The chatbot provides automated answers to financial questions posed by its users using natural language processing and IBM Watson. Admins can update the bot's corpus and crawl through sources using Apache Lucene. Users can also search through the bot's index to find more information.
